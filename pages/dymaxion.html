The Igneous Dymaxion is a new projection of the Earth looking at the planet from the lens of the Earth Crust: the most outer layer of the planet, continuous across the depths of the oceans and surface of the continents. Broken in 7 main tectonic plates, the Earth Crust is seemed back through the tectonic faults. The Igneous Dymaxion describes the Earth through the tectonic faults that fuel the geological Igneous Cycle.
